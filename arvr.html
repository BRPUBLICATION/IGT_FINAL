Program Curriculum

Semester 1: Foundations of AR/VR

  • Introduction to AR/VR (4 credits): Overview, history, trends, and applications.
  • 3D Modeling and Animation (4 credits): Blender, animation principles.
  • Programming for AR/VR (4 credits): C#, Java, Python, Unity, Unreal Engine.
  • User Experience Design (4 credits): UX principles, human-centered design.
  • AR/VR Development Fundamentals (4 credits): Hardware understanding, immersive experience design.

Semester 2: Advanced AR/VR Development

  • Advanced AR/VR Development (4 credits): AI/ML integration, performance optimization.
  • Project Management and Teamwork (4 credits): Agile, team collaboration.
  • Specialized AR/VR Topics (4 credits): Healthcare, education, emerging trends.
  • Capstone Project (8 credits): Portfolio development, real-world application.
  • Internship and Industry Exposure (4 credits): Hands-on development, industry networking.
