Program Curriculum

Semester 1: Foundations of AR/VR

  • Introduction to AR/VR: History, trends, applications. (4 credits)
  • 3D Modeling & Animation: Blender, animation principles. (4 credits)
  • Programming for AR/VR: C#, Java, Python, Unity, Unreal Engine. (4 credits)
  • User Experience Design: UX principles & tools. (4 credits)
  • AR/VR Development Fundamentals: Hardware, immersive design. (4 credits)

Semester 2: Advanced AR/VR Development

  • Advanced AR/VR Techniques: AI/ML integration. (4 credits)
  • Project Management & Teamwork: Agile, collaboration. (4 credits)
  • Specialized Topics: AR/VR in healthcare, education. (4 credits)
  • Capstone Project: Portfolio-based development. (8 credits)
  • Internship & Industry Exposure: Hands-on AR/VR training. (4 credits)
